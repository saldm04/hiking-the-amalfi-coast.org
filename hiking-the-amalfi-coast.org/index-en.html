Who we are

We are a non-profit association founded to protect, develop and enhance the fragile territory of the Amalfi Coast through hiking and the promotion of authentic, sustainable tourism. This land is more than just the sea: it is also mountains, trails and a thousand-year-old history that deserves to be rediscovered and preserved.

We organise open days to explore the paths, volunteer days to care for hiking routes and public green areas, and events that celebrate local traditions and the historical heritage of the former maritime republic. We believe that safeguarding the territory starts with knowledge and respect for its natural balance, encouraging greater awareness of how important it is to protect it in every form.

Our mission is to contribute actively to the protection of the Amalfi Coast by promoting initiatives that highlight every aspect of the area, from the shoreline to the hilltops, while respecting its identity and history.
